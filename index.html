Who We Are

JMJ Business Process Outsourcing is a Sole Proprietorship company based in the Philippines. The company was established by our very own CEO that has been in the industry for 15 years. Our company is best known for its ability to produce Customer Service, talents specializing Technical in Support, Appointment Setting, Lead Generation and Admin tasks. We are well known for our loyalty and ability to always go the extra mile to maintain a good relationship with our clients as well as its consumers. JMJ is founded with passion, hard work and commitment to always give only the best and high quality services. We are proud to present our purpose - to provide job opportunities for most talented Filipinos that have been affected by the pandemic and to give high quality of services to the clients globally.
